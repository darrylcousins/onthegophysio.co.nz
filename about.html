About On the Go Physio

On the Go Physio is a small but expanding team of physiotherapists with a passion for providing high quality therapy in a person's own environment.

On the Go Physio works to the following guiding values.

  • Complete respect for the clients who allow us into their homes
  • Our clients are the experts in knowing what they need. We are there to share our expertise but we are not 'the boss!'
  • Respect for the fact that people are funding their treatment themselves. We will not encourage extra visits that are not essential and we will always inform you if you could be getting your physiotherapy for free from the DHB or another Health and Disability service.
  • We will always try to be flexible. We offer out of hours appointments if family members would like to be present.
  • With our clients consent we stay in close contact with their GPs to keep them informed of progress. We work in harmony with other health and care professionals involved with our clients.
  • We have a network of other health professionals and equipment suppliers that we can recommend with confidence
