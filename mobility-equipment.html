Scooters, Wheelchairs and other equipment

Mechanics need tools, mountain climbers need tools and sometimes people with mobility restrictions need tools too! From a simple walking stick right through to a high end mobility scooter or wheelchair different aids can be useful to keep you safe and allow you the freedom to move. On the Go physio can assess you in your own environment and advise you on what aids will best suit your level of ability and lifestyle. We can also advise on funding options as you may be eligible for equipment through the Ministry of Health or to apply for lottery funding.
